FINAL: The technique that allows models to break down complex logic into step-by-step derivations is known as **Chain-of-Thought (CoT) prompting** (or reasoning).

Instead of forcing a model to jump directly from a prompt to a final answer, this approach encourages the model to generate a series of intermediate reasoning steps. By articulating these logical steps, the model can decompose multi-step problems into more manageable components, which significantly improves performance on tasks requiring arithmetic, commonsense reasoning, and symbolic manipulation. This process effectively creates a "chain" of thought that makes the model's internal logic more transparent and accurate before it arrives at the final conclusion.
